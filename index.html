about

I have a passion for creative marketing and seeing interesting and innovative start-up ideas succeed. It's seeing the fruition and being part of the fulfilment of a project that motivates me. I am a perpetual learner, which lends itself to the rapidly evolving industry of marketing. It's crucial for a marketer to perform an array of tasks: branding, SEO, web design, data metrics, A/B testing, CRM. I have devoted many years to practising all of these and am proud to claim this full-stack marketing outfit with certifications from Google & Hubspot. I enjoy using this skill set to help start-ups succeed.

Indulging in the more creative side of this skill set has enabled me to practise my other passion; grahic design. I have worked on many projects solely as a designer for web and print. You can find a collection of my commercial work and personal projects on social sites Behance & Dribbble. You can catch a partial view of my photography attempts on Instagram.
